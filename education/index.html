Overview

Educational meetings provide students with a guided learning experience inside the zoo. These sessions include a tour led by a knowledgeable guide, interactive discussions, and animal fact sheets tailored to each area visited. Students also get the chance to meet a zoo keeper who shares insights about animal care and conservation. The goal of these meetings is to make learning engaging, hands-on, and connected to real wildlife.
